ONLINE VOTING

Online voting, or e-voting, allows individuals to cast their votes via secure internet platforms, enhancing convenience and accessibility. This method can increase voter participation by allowing people to vote from anywhere. However, it raises significant concerns regarding security, privacy, and the digital divide, potentially disenfranchising those without reliable internet access. Successful implementation requires secure authentication, encryption, and robust audit trails.
